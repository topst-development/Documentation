# 1. <span style="color:green"> Underdogs in Basketball Field </span>
## 1-1. Kawamura Yuki(172cm)
![Image](https://cdn.rookie.co.kr/news/photo/202410/101455_120701_1245.jpg "Kawamura Yuki")
He is born in 2001, Japan. His height is 172cm, but he made a successful debut in Memphis Grizzles, NBA. He became a role-model of point-guards whose height is short. The sensational play is that he made a 3 point shot at FIBA against Lauri Markkanen, Finnish NBA player in Utah Jazz.
